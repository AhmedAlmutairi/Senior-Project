# read me!

engOrg is team for the class CS461/CS462 which is a senior project for 2016/2017 at Western Oregon University. 

## What is myWall?
It is an application to collaborate with your team, your co-worker, your friends, and other people that you share the same interest with. In myWall app, you can have a good and an appropriate environment to be connected with your social. This app provides several tools that are easy to use and useful for your needs such as draw diagram, making lists, posting documents, ...etc. The focus is to have an amazing place to share your ideas, comments, knowledge, and other things with the right people in the right time.
