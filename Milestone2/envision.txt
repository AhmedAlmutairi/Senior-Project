Envision Statement: 

Team engOrg envisions Headline-Hut to be a positive environment, where creative people can go on to learn, and explore different genres of materials with little effort. Headline-Hut will be structured to be user friendly that way Headline-Hut enthusiasts will be able to use this online web application with extreme ease. Admins will be able to cancel any headline they may find inappropriate, as well as approve any headline they feel is fit to be on Headline-Hut. Headline-Hut will make headline browsing an easy task, and will make great suggestions of new headlines to check out. Team engOrg's goal is to provide a happy experience browsing and reading up on good quality articles.
